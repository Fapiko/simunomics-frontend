Simunomics is a massive-multiplayer Business Simulation Game where you create your own business and set your own strategy to conquer a vast, changing economy.

  • Choose one product or many.
  • Build Farms, Factories, Stores, Research & Development Centers, and/or Raw Materials sites.
  • Set prices for retail customers, or deal purely with other businesses.
  • Create your own products and name your own brands, or be a reseller of someone else's.
  • Earn awards and try to be the most decorated company, or focus on the bottom line and go purely for size.
  • Follow your progress with graphs that track market share and price movements, and accounting statements that follow GAAP and IFRS standards..
  • Be part of an economy that grows and changes with you.

Fun meets realism in a virtual world. Simunomics is the Ultimate Economy Game.
